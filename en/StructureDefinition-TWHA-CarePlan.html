Resource Profile: 健康檢查適性配工計畫 Profile

Official URL: https://twcore.mohw.gov.tw/ig/twha/StructureDefinition/TWHA-CarePlan Version: 0.1.0
Active as of 2026-06-23 Computable Name: TWHACarePlanProfile

用於針對第四級健康管理勞工，由醫師、事業單位等共同制定之適性配工計畫（變更場所、工作或縮短工時等），繼承自 TW Core CarePlan。

Usages:

  • This Profile is not used by any profiles in this Specification

You can also check for usages in the FHIR IG Statistics
